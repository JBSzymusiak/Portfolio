About

I am a dancer, performer, choreographer and movement educator based in Poland. Dance is a space for exploring without the need to find answers. My practice combines physical training, improvisation and animalistic inspirations. I enjoy the precision of choreography — detail, intention and multi‑layered movement give me a sense of order and beauty.

  • Certified Contemporary Dance Teacher, NCK Kraków (2010–2012)
