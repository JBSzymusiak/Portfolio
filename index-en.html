Joanna Brodniak‑Szymusiak
Portfolio

Dancer, performer, choreographer and movement educator based in Poland. I’m interested in what moves us and organically supports our motivation to move. My practice blends physical training, improvisation and animalistic inspirations; the precision of choreography — detail, intention and multi-layered movement — gives me a sense of order and beauty.
